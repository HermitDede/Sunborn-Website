Equipment System

Characters will have 10 slots for equipment; Head, Necklace, Body, Right Hand, Left Hand, Glove, Belt, Leggings, Boots and 2 ring slots. In town, basic items can be purchased for these slots, but good quality equipment will randomly generated and placed on each level. Equipment will have quality types such as common, uncommon, rare, unique and legendary. Artifacts and relics will have same quality types and depending on the quality, they can be sold for more money or displayed for more tourist attractions.



Current alpha version includes 3 types of rarities; Common, Uncommon, Rare. Common equipment has not extra benefits, but uncommon and rare equipment has various number of benefits depending on the rarity. Each benefit coming from equipment also changes values depending on the the level of the equipment and the chance factor during the generation. Battle System

Exploring and fighting system will be turn-based. Once the player makes an action, one turn will pass for player and every enemy on the map at same time. After every turn, the player will see feedback visually and via game log about the turn such as damages done on himself or enemies, who dies or disappears etc.

If an enemy gets into sight, battle mode turns on and shows available actions with different colors such as blue means skipping the turn, red means attack and green means move to tile selected. Below, you can see an example of it.
